History

Founded In 2006, We Started Service With 6 Farmers And Two Employees.Farms First pvt.Ltd. Has Taken Over The Entire Business – Management & Operations – In August, 2013. Since Then, We Have Grown In Both – Size And Service Offerings, Becoming Well Known In Pune, Pimpri-Chinchwad And NOW Navi-Mumbai, As A Quality Home Farm Product Supplier. Nearly 75% Of Our Business Comes From Existing Registered Customers.

Farm-Born Values

Farmers are the ultimate entrepreneurs managing complex businesses on their own when the odds are often stacked against them. Farmers Market strives to have the same values and work ethic as the farmer entrepreneurs in the network. Many Farmers Market team members are farmers, grew up on farms, or have a deep connection to the land, so this is personal.

Every farmer is a member of Farmers Market, resulting in economically viable and sustainable family farms and rural communities.

Act like an owner, be a great team, serve Family Farmers.

Farmers Market is      on a mission to create a future of farming that puts Farmers First by democratizing information, providing unbiased analytics and creating competition for farmers’ business.
